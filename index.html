Founded in 2019 and officially operational since 2020, GMN is located in An Binh Ward, Binh Tan, Bien Hoa City, Dong Nai Province. We specialize in the production of OE yarn and supply raw materials for the textile industry, sourcing high-quality materials from leading countries such as Australia and Brazil, as well as utilizing a stable supply of domestic resources. Our products serve as the primary raw material for textile companies both in Vietnam and abroad.

Vision

With high-quality and consistent yarn, we strive to become a trusted partner in the textile industry, building a prosperous future for both Vietnam and the global market.

Mission

GMN is committed to sustainable development, balancing economic benefits, social responsibility, and environmental protection. Our mission is to provide high-quality, eco-friendly yarn products, contributing to the sustainable growth of the textile industry and the community.

Operating Principles

Good Practices: Protecting the rights of employees, customers, and partners is GMN's top priority, promoting sustainable economic growth through trade activities and supplying high-quality textile materials.

Innovation and Improvement: GMN continuously innovates and improves, delivering new values based on a solid technological foundation.

Responsibility: We prioritize providing products with clear origins that are environmentally friendly, encouraging sustainable consumption and production in the textile industry.

Sustainability: We implement measures to minimize environmental impact, using energy efficiently and contributing to climate change mitigation.

Collaboration for Goals: We build strong partnerships with stakeholders, sharing knowledge and experience to continuously create new value together.
